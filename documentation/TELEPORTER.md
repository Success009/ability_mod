# Class Documentation: Teleporter

**Role:** Mobility
**Status:** **POLISHED & FUNCTIONAL**
**Key Mechanic:** Spatial Displacement & Rifts

## 1. Overview
The Teleporter is the master of space and distance, capable of blinking through the world and establishing persistent warp points for themselves and their allies.

## 2. Active Abilities

### **Blink (Phase 2 Navigation)**
Instantly displace yourself to a targeted location.
*   **Activation:** Hold **Sneak (Shift)** and press **Swap Item (F)** while looking at a distant block.
*   **Cost:** 2 Levels per use.
*   **Cooldown:** 5 Seconds.
*   **Safety:** Checks for obstructions to ensure a safe landing.

### **The Network (Rift System)**
Open the fabric of space to signal players or warp to anchors.
*   **Activation:** **Look Down + Sneak (Shift) + Swap Item (F)**.
*   **Interface:** Opens a chat-based ritual menu.
*   **Options:**
    *   **Player Signals:** Locate other players and request a Warp TO them or a SUMMON to you.
    *   **Warp Points:** Manage up to 10 personal anchor points.
*   **Cooldown:** 30 Seconds (Network Pulse) / 5 Minutes (Warp Point Usage).

### **Request & Suspense System**
Cross-player teleports require mutual consent.
*   **Consent:** Target players must accept the warp request via a clickable chat prompt.
*   **Channeling:** Once accepted, a 4-second "Suspense Sequence" begins with visual and auditory cues before the rift opens.

## 3. Constraints
*   **Combat Lock:** All teleportation (except Blink) is strictly prohibited while in combat.
*   **Movement:** Moving while in the Network menu or during the warp suspense will shatter the rift.
*   **Mana:** Higher-distance player signals cost more XP levels.

## 4. Scoreboard Integration
All Teleporter cooldowns (Blink, Network, Warp CD) are displayed on the high-fidelity sidebar with precision time formatting.
